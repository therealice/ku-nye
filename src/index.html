Ku nye

Traditional tibetan massage

What is Ku nye?

Ku nye is a tibetan healing massage and an integral part of Tibetan Medicine. The Tibetan word Ku (སྐུ་) means "to apply oil", and the word nye (མཉེ་) means "to use pressure with movement".

What you can expect

You can expect a gentle massage with massage oil. Light pressure will be applied on certain points. We focus on giving you a sense of wellbeing. Both the front and the back side of your body will be massaged. The treatment lasts for about 90 minuttes.
