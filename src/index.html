What is Ku Nye?

Ku Nye (སྐུ་མཉེ།) is a Tibetan form of massage, which has been practiced for thousands of years in Tibet and the Himalayan region of Asia as an integral part of Tibetan Medicine. In Tibetan, the word Ku (སྐུ་) means to apply oil and Nye (མཉེ་)་means to apply pressure with a variety of movements.

Treatment

During our Ku Nye treatment, we apply oil with pressure on both front and back side of the body.
