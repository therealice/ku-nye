What is Ku Nye?

Ku Nye is a tibetan healing massage and an integral part of Tibetan Medicine. The Tibetan word Ku (སྐུ་) means "to apply oil", and the word Nye (མཉེ་) means "to use pressure with movement". Since ancient times in Tibet, it has been believed that Ku Nye aids in longevity by clearing and sharpening the sensory organs while supporting the function of the internal organs.

What you can expect

You can expect a gentle massage with massage oil. Your neck, back, legs and feet will be massaged. Only the area of your body being worked on is uncovered. Hopefully you will feel relaxed and experience a sense of well-being.
