What is Ku nye?

Ku nye is a tibetan healing massage and an integral part of Tibetan Medicine which has been practiced for thousands of years in Tibet and the Himalayan region of Asia. In Tibetan, the word Ku (སྐུ་) means to apply oil, and the word nye (མཉེ་) means to use pressure with movement.

What you can expect

You can expect a gentle massage with natural tibetan massage oil. Some pressure will be applied on certain points. We focus on giving you a sense of peace so you feel relaxed. Both the front and the back of your body will be massaged. The treatment lasts for about 90 minuttes.
